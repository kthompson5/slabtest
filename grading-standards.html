Grading Standards

We grade on a 1–10 scale for each category on Front and Back:

  • Surface (S)
  • Centering (C)
  • Edges (E) — average of 4 edges
  • Corners (K) — average of 4 corners

Formula:

Front = (S_f + C_f + E_f + K_f) / 4
Back  = (S_b + C_b + E_b + K_b) / 4
SM    = 0.80 × Front + 0.20 × Back
