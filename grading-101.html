Card Grading 101

Before You Submit

  • Penny sleeve & Card Saver
  • Clean surface gently; avoid scratches
  • Protect corners & edges during packing
  • Include printed submission summary

Common Defects

  • Surface lines, dimples
  • Off-centering
  • Corner whitening
  • Edge chipping
